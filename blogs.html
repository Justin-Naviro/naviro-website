UNDERSTANDING THE HOMELESS COMMUNITIES IN CAPE TOWN

16 FEBRUARY 2024

Cape Town, one of South Africa's most vibrant cities, is home to a diverse population, including a significant homeless community. Despite its breathtaking natural beauty and cultural richness, Cape Town struggles with socio-economic disparities that have led to an increasing number of individuals experiencing homelessness. In this blog post, we will shed light on the challenges faced by the homeless community in Cape Town and discuss their pressing needs, urging for collective efforts to address this humanitarian crisis.
