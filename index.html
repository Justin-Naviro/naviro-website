ABOUT US

OUR MISSION

Raise Awareness

Through social media, education, and grassroots efforts, the goal is to shed light on the realities faced by individuals experiencing homelessness. Our mission aims to inspire compassion and collective action, emphasising the need for sustainable solutions to address this pressing societal issue.

Challenge Stigmas

Naviro aims to break stereotypes and foster compassion through awareness campaigns and education. We seek to humanise and de-stigmatise the experiences of those facing homelessness, encouraging empathy and dismantling harmful and ill judgments.

Foster Empathy

Focusing on cultivating understanding through storytelling and community engagement, Naviro aims to highlight personal narratives, evoke compassion and break down stereotypes, fostering a more empathetic society committed to addressing the root causes of homelessness.

    Self-Sufficient Community

    This initiative focuses on creating self-sufficient communities for homeless individuals, providing a supportive environment for them to rebuild their lives with dignity and purpose.

    At the heart of this programme is our commitment to securing fertile land, offering a foundation for sustainable living. By providing a stable environment and comprehensive training programs covering skills from agriculture to vocational expertise, we empower residents to cultivate a sense of belonging and ownership.

    Central to our approach is the revival of a family-type structure within the community. Fostering a nurturing atmosphere of togetherness, we encourage residents to support each other in personal and collective growth. Through a thoughtful recruitment process, we identify natural leaders and assign roles that align with their strengths, fostering a sense of purpose and responsibility. This is a holistic journey towards rebuilding lives and creating lasting change. Join us in this empowering journey, where everyone deserves the opportunity to thrive, and together, we can build a future filled with hope, resilience, and self-sufficiency.

  • Care Packages

    31 March 2024

    Care Packages

    Our project is centered around creating comprehensive care packages specifically designed to address the essential needs of individuals experiencing homelessness.

    These thoughtfully curated packages include a variety of crucial items, such as hygiene products, warm clothing, non-perishable food, and informative materials about local resources. Our primary goal is to provide immediate support, restore dignity, and foster resilience among those facing homelessness.

    Through these care packages, we aim to make a substantial and lasting impact on the lives of individuals in need, offering them essential resources and a renewed sense of hope for a brighter future.

  • Animal Sanctuary

    Coming Soon

    Animal Sanctuary

    Our commitment to compassion extends to our furry friends through this transformative project. Dedicated to rescuing homeless animals from the streets and safeguarding them from abandonment and health risks, our sanctuary provides a lifeline for these dependent companions.

    At the core of this initiative is a state-of-the-art sanctuary, equipped with all the necessities including on-site veterinarians and grooming rooms, ensuring every resident's safety and comfort. Whether they find forever homes or not, our goal is to offer these animals a haven to enjoy the rest of their lives.

    Beyond traditional shelters, we introduce unique attractions such as animal cafes and petting rooms, creating an engaging experience for visitors and fostering awareness about the significance of animal welfare. Join us in building a world where every animal finds its haven, and together, let's provide these loving creatures with a second chance at a life filled with love and care.
